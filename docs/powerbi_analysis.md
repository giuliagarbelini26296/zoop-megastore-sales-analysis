# Power BI Dashboard

## Objective

Develop an executive dashboard to support business decision-making.

## KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Average Revenue per Customer

## Visualizations

* Monthly Revenue Trend
* Top 5 Products
* Revenue by State
* Revenue by Category
* Prime vs Non-Prime Comparison

## Business Value

The dashboard allows managers to quickly identify:

* Best-performing products
* Most valuable customers
* Regional sales opportunities
* Customer segment performance
