# SQL Analysis

## Objective

Answer business questions through SQL queries.

## Queries Developed

### Top 5 Products by Revenue

Identified the products with the highest revenue contribution.

### Top 5 Customers by Spending

Ranked customers according to total spending.

### Prime vs Non-Prime Customers

Compared average revenue generated by each customer group.

### Revenue by Category

Analyzed category-level performance.

## Main Findings

* Digital Drawing Tablet generated the highest revenue.
* Gabriela Lima was the highest-spending customer.
* Non-Prime customers generated higher average revenue than Prime customers.
